How to Delete Your Account

To request deletion of your Bharat Kisan account and all associated data, please send an email to:

📧 bharatkisan.support@gmail.com

Include the following in your email:

  1. Your registered phone number or username
  2. Your registered email address (if signed up with Google)

What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, phone number, email)
  • All price alerts you created
  • Your saved preferences and settings
  • Push notification tokens

Account deletion is typically processed within 7 business days.
